ɳClaw is a personal AI assistant with infinite, self-organizing memory. It remembers every conversation, extracts facts, decisions, and entities, and builds a knowledge graph that compounds over time.

Unlike other AI chat apps, ɳClaw does not start you with "New Chat" every time. Your sidebar shows your life organized by topics. You just talk. It auto-detects subjects, branches conversations when you shift context, and connects related ideas across months of dialogue.

Your data stays on YOUR server. ɳClaw is fully self-hosted. You run the backend on your own hardware or VPS using the nSelf CLI. No cloud accounts, no data mining, no vendor lock-in.

KEY FEATURES

Personal memory that grows
Every conversation builds your personal knowledge base. ɳClaw extracts names, dates, preferences, project details, and decisions. Ask "what did I decide about the kitchen renovation?" six months later and get an instant, sourced answer.

Topic-based organization
Conversations auto-organize into topics. Your sidebar shows "Work / Project Alpha", "Health / Running", "Finance / Budget 2026" instead of a wall of "New Chat" entries.

Context that follows you
Switch between topics freely. ɳClaw carries relevant context forward. Start talking about a recipe, mention your grocery budget, and it already knows your monthly food spending from a conversation last week.

Voice and text
Speak naturally or type. Real-time transcription with support for multiple languages. Voice replies when your hands are busy.

Share anything into ɳClaw
Share articles, images, or text from any app directly into ɳClaw. It reads, summarizes, and files the content into the right topic automatically.

End-to-end encrypted
All data encrypted with X25519 + XChaCha20-Poly1305. Your server, your keys, your data.

Self-hosted backend
Powered by nSelf CLI. PostgreSQL stores everything (pgvector for semantic search, ltree for topic trees, JSONB for flexible schemas). Redis for speed. MeiliSearch for full-text search.

Cross-platform
Available on iOS, Android, macOS, and web. All clients sync through your self-hosted backend.
